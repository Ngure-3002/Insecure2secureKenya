Frequently Asked Questions

×
What is Insecure2Secure?
Insecure2Secure is a self-help platform designed to help individuals overcome personal insecurities through practical, trustworthy information and resources.
How do the subscription plans work?
We offer flexible plans ranging from hourly to annual subscriptions. Each plan provides access to our content library, with different durations and features to suit your needs.
What payment methods do you accept?
We currently accept M-Pesa payments for all our subscription plans. Payment is secure and confirmation is instant.
Can I cancel my subscription?
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. However, we do not offer refunds for the current subscription period.
How can I get support?
You can reach our support team via email, phone, or WhatsApp during business hours. Check our Contact Us section for details.
